What Is Pakhomo Jobs?

An online platform connecting trained domestic and hospitality workers with households, restaurants, lodges, and offices looking for reliable help.

🏠

For Clients

Book trusted helpers like maids, cleaners, waiters, or cooks with ease. View profiles, manage schedules, and handle payments securely.

👩🏾‍🔧

For Workers

Find dignified work opportunities, access training resources, and build your reputation through our rating system.

🛠️

Admin Backend

Comprehensive management system for approving workers, handling bookings, managing payments, and ensuring quality.

📚

Trust & Education

Transparent training processes, verification systems, and community guidelines that build trust and safety.
