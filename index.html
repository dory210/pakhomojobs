Your Journey with Pakhomo Jobs

As a Client

  1. Create your account and complete verification
  2. Browse available workers in your area
  3. View profiles, ratings, and previous work
  4. Book your preferred worker for specific dates
  5. Communicate directly through our platform
  6. Enjoy quality service at your location
  7. Rate and review your experience

As a Worker

  1. Apply through our registration form
  2. Complete identity verification and background check
  3. Attend our training programs (in-person/online)
  4. Get approved and create your professional profile
  5. Receive job notifications and accept bookings
  6. Provide excellent service and build ratings
  7. Grow your income and client base
